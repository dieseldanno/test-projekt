WE ARE THE MIST

In 2023, The Mist Perfumery, a perfume company, emerged with a profound fascination for Nordic mythology and the natural beauty of the North. Founded by Astrid Viklund and Erik Björnsson, their fragrances are an olfactory journey through Nordic landscapes.

Drawing inspiration from Nordic mythology, their scents are named after Norse deities, capturing the essence of the North. With a commitment to sustainability, The Mist Perfumery quickly gained recognition for transporting wearers to the enchanting world of the North.
